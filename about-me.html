Who I Am

I am here,
because I'm not all there.

Heil!

I am Leif Harstad, direct Norwegian descendant. Guided by ancestral memory, I craft bind-runes and sacred symbols into stone - awakening their ancient power to shield those who carry them.

As shield-bearer to two warrior daughters who kindle my hearth-fire, I stand between worlds - honouring ancient wisdom while facing modern struggles. Through intention, practice, and patient concentration, I imbue each stone with protective magic that speaks to today's challenges.

Each piece that passes through my hands carries both the whispered wisdom of the Elders and a father's blessing - for what greater magic exists than that which protects those we hold dear? These are not simply crafts. They are bridges between the old ways and new beginnings, forged for anyone seeking strength in their personal saga.

Like the ancient skalds who kept wisdom alive through the longest winters, my divergent mind and steadfast heart work to kindle flames of magic in these shadow-filled times. For in each rune I carve, a small piece of Mimir's ancient light returns to our realm - exactly when it is needed most.
