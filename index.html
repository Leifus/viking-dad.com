What I Do

The Sacred Process

Powerful protective amulets created through my own Norse ritual - transforming raw stone into bind-runes that shield carriers from modern world chaos.
ᛒ Find Out More

Stone
Harvesting

Each stone is carefully selected and harvested from ancient waterfalls and rivers that have flowed since the time of the first skalds.

Rune
Selection

Based on the stone's natural energy and shape, I select the appropriate runes to creates a powerful bind-rune that amplifies the protective qualities.

Carving
& Colouring

I hand-carve the bind-rune into the stone. Each stroke is made with purpose and intention. A colour is then chosen and applied.

Ritual
Sealing

At an ancient burial site, the bind-runes undergo ritual oiling and sealing, awakening their dormant powers.

Scribing
& Shielding

The meaning and intent is transcribed by hand. Finally, the stone is shielded in protective wrapping to preserve the concentrated energies absorbed.
